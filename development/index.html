Top 5 Episodes

  • "Pilot" (Season 1, Episode 1) - The episode that introduced us to the world of "Gossip Girl" and the scandalous lives of the Upper East Side elite.
  • "Victor/Victrola" (Season 1, Episode 7) - Blair's romance with Chuck takes a steamy turn at a burlesque club.
  • "Last Tango, Then Paris" (Season 3, Episode 22) - Chuck and Blair's tumultuous relationship reaches a turning point in the season finale.
  • "The Unblairable Lightness of Being" (Season 4, Episode 1) - Blair struggles to move on from Chuck while Serena adjusts to her new life.
  • "New York, I Love You XOXO" (Season 6, Episode 10) - The series finale brings closure to the characters' storylines and reveals the identity of Gossip Girl.
